BON APPÉTIT

This is a re-design of an existing American magazine, Bon Appétit. The re-design consists of a subscription card and a mult-page editorial feature, with a goal of exploring a new visual direction.

ROLE:

Graphic and Visual Design

For this project, I had to create an art direction that has not yet been done for Bon Appétit, while ensuring that it stays on-brand. I explored the different design styles that Bon Appétit currently does, as it differs on print vs. online.

TOOLS:

Sketch, InDesign

SUBSCRIPTION CARD

WHAT:

The subscription card incorporated a clear branding for Bon Appétit's design aesthetic and a distinctive call-to-action subscription offer.

WHY:

In order to capture the reader's attention, I used eye-catching color and imagery to highlight the important content of the card.

The yellow acts as an accent colour to highlight the most important call to action areas.

MULTI-PAGE EDITORIAL FEATURE

WHAT:

The multi-page editorial feature is re-designed to include bonus editorial content.

WHY:

The yellow accents acts as visual hierarchy to guide the reader's eyes to move along the page. This also includes the bold numbering on the second editorial.

Because the original editorial feature only showcased images of the food (short ribs) and very minimal text, I took this as an opportunity to add on content of showcasing different methods of cooking the ribs.
