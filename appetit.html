Bon Appétit

This is a re-design of an existing American magazine, Bon Appétit. The re-design consists of a readership survey, a subscription card, and a mult-page editorial feature.

Role:

Graphic and visual design

Readership Survey

The readership survey consists of a thirty-question readership survey that will accurately identify the publication’s readership to gain insights on their demographics and psychographics.

To ensure that this survey is designed on-brand, I examined Bon Appétit's current design aesthetic and applied that onto the structure of the survey.

I had to consider the typographic hierarchy to guide readers on the next important element on the page. Mirco-typography was also closely examined as a lot of information has to fit in the enclosed space and has to be legible for the reader.

Subscription Card

The subscription card required clear branding for Bon Appétit's design aesthetic and a distinctive call-to-action subscription offer.

In order to capture the reader's attention, I used eye-catching color and imagery to highlight the important content of the card.

Multi-page Editorial Feature

The multi-page editorial feature is re-designed to include bonus editorial content.

The yellow accents acts as visual hierarchy to guide the reader's eyes to move along the page. This also includes the bold numbering on the second editorial. Because the original editorial feature only showcased images of the food (short ribs) and very minimal text, I took this as an opportunity to add on content of showcasing different methods of cooking the ribs.
